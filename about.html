The Story

Why Schoobrary exists.

This page uses an editorial story flow instead of repeated cards.

1

The physical library problem

For decades, school libraries have been shaped by limited budgets, limited space, and a limited number of physical copies. Even well-funded schools struggle to expand access for every student.

2

The imported digital problem

Many digital solutions are imported, English-first, and disconnected from teacher workflows. They do not always understand lesson plans, curriculum alignment, or teacher control.

3

The Schoobrary answer

Schoobrary was built around a simple premise: the teacher is the pedagogical authority, the school is the institutional home, and the content must serve the curriculum.

Our Mission

To make every book, every lesson, and every learning resource a school could need available to every student — under the teacher's control, inside the school's workflow.
