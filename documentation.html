A practical guide to the public website pages, content sections, product messages, integrations, and operating links.

Site Map

Main public pages.

The website is organized around product explanation, audience-specific value, content libraries, assessment, integrations, and contact workflows.

Home

Introduces Schoobrary as a fully integrated digital school library, explains the core value proposition, and links to key product areas.

About

Explains why Schoobrary exists, the regional need it addresses, and its connection to Almotahida Education Group.

Libraries

Documents the twelve specialized content libraries and how resources are connected through metadata and curriculum mapping.

Question Bank

Explains smart assessment, automatic grading, tagged questions, difficulty levels, Bloom's taxonomy, and remediation support.

Integrations

Summarizes LMS, SSO, LTI, SCORM, xAPI, and the technical review points schools may need before adoption.

Contact

Collects school, role, student count, and message details. Submissions are directed to the Schoobrary contact email workflow.

Operational Notes

Current live links and workflows.

The public website is static HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Updates are committed to the GitHub repository and published from the main branch.

Login buttons point to https://library.schoobrary.com.
Demo and inquiry workflows point users to the contact page.
The contact form uses a mail workflow directed to info@scoobrary.com.
English and Arabic pages are maintained as separate HTML files with matched page structure.
Shared styling, responsive rules, and 3D icon styling are managed in assets/css/styles.css.
