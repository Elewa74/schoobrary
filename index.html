What is Schoobrary

A digital library, redesigned for how schools actually work.

Schoobrary is not a content marketplace. It is not a book-rental service. It is a purpose-built digital school library that unifies twelve specialized content collections — books, videos, 3D models, assessments, lesson plans, and more — under a single platform.

Why Schoobrary

Built on three uncompromising principles.

Teacher control, curriculum alignment, and integration are at the center of the experience.

Teacher-controlled

Every resource is gated by the teacher. Nothing reaches a student without being part of a lesson plan or an explicit assignment. The teacher remains the pedagogical authority at all times.

Learn more →

Curriculum-aligned

Every asset is tagged to a specific learning outcome in a specific national curriculum. Search returns exactly what the lesson needs — no noise, no guesswork, no wasted planning time.

Learn more →

Integrated by design

Native support for LTI, SCORM, and xAPI. Single sign-on with your school's existing identity provider. Works inside your current LMS, not alongside it.

Learn more →
